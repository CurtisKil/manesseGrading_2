Who We Are

35 years ago, Paul Manesse started as a laborer. Then worked his way up to operator, grade checker, foreman, superintendent, and now as the owner of Manesse Grading. He still loves getting his hands dirty so you can find him on the ground working with his skilled team.

About Us

We do small projects, large projects, and, or, consultation. We work along side the geologists and surveyors to address problems and find solutions to move your project along at a fluid pace. We love taking on the more complicated jobs, but will bring the same passion towards the simple ones too.
